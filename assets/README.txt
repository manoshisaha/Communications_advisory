BACKGROUND IMAGES FOR "WHAT SETS US APART" SECTION
====================================================
Add these four images to this folder:

  why-evidence.jpg    — Card 01: We Lead with Evidence
  why-strategy.jpg    — Card 02: We Think Like Strategists
  why-market.jpg      — Card 03: We Know This Market
  why-discretion.jpg  — Card 04: We Protect Discretion

Recommended: dark/moody photography, at least 800x600px.
The images appear at 10% opacity on hover over the red background.
Any photo works — the overlay keeps text readable.

SECTION BACKGROUND IMAGES
==========================
Create a "sections/" subfolder and add:
  sections/about-bg.jpg
  sections/mission-bg.jpg
  sections/contact-bg.jpg

Then add to the relevant section tag in index.html:
  data-bg="assets/sections/about-bg.jpg"
  data-bg-opacity="0.07"
