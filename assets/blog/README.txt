Blog article images are uploaded here automatically when you use the CMS at /admin.

You can also add images manually:
- Recommended size: 1200 x 630px (standard article thumbnail ratio)
- Format: JPG or WebP preferred for smaller file sizes
- Naming: match your post slug e.g. crisis-communications.jpg

Reference them in your Markdown posts as:
image: /assets/blog/your-image.jpg
