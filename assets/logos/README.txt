Place partner logo images in this folder.

Supported formats: PNG, SVG, JPG
Recommended size: at least 300px wide, transparent background preferred

Naming convention: use-lowercase-with-hyphens.png
Example: brac.png, grameen-bank.png, dutch-bangla.png

After adding a logo, update index.html:
<div class="logo-item"><img src="assets/logos/your-logo.png" alt="Organisation Name"></div>
